Hi, I'm Alan Turing

ABOUT ME

My name is Alan Turing (as you might already know), but my full name is Alan Mathison Turing (did you know that? Ha!). I was born on June 23, 1912, in Maida Vale, London and I am a British mathematician and logician, who has made major contributions to mathematics, cryptanalysis, logic, philosophy, and mathematical biology and also to computer science, cognitive science, artificial intelligence, and artificial life. I'm going to tell you about all of that, so stick with me till the end.

Qualification

I did my primary education at Hazelhurst Preparatory School, an independent school in the village of Frant in Sussex. At the age of 13, I shifted to Sherborne School, a boarding independent school in the market town of Sherborne in Dorset. I graduated in 1934 from King's College, Cambridge, with a first-class honours degree and as a result of his dissertation, was elected a Fellow of King's College at the age of 22. In 1936, I went on to study mathematics at Princeton University, New Jersey, obtaining a Ph. D. in 1938.
